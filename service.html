SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT

Software development is the process of creating, designing, testing, and maintaining computer programs and applications to meet user needs or business goals.This comprehensive process goes beyond just coding and includes all steps from initial concept and requirements analysis to deployment and ongoing maintenance, often following a structured software development lifecycle (SDLC).It requires a combination of computer science principles, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

Ai/Ml

AI, or artificial intelligence, is the broad concept of creating machines that can simulate human intelligence, while ML, or machine learning, is a specific subset of AI where algorithms learn from data to perform tasks without being explicitly programmed. AI includes any system that mimics human cognitive functions like problem-solving and perception, whereas ML uses statistical methods to find patterns in data to make predictions or decisions.

Data Analysis

A data analyst gathers, cleans, and interprets raw data to identify trends, predict outcomes, and provide actionable insights that help organizations make better business decisions. They use tools like SQL, Excel, and Power BI or Tableau to analyze data, create visualizations like charts and dashboards, and communicate their findings to stakeholders through reports and presentations
